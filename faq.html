Product & Technology

A steam oven uses steam heat to cook food, rather than relying solely on dry hot air. Water is heated to produce steam, which circulates throughout the oven cavity. This method of cooking preserves nutrients, moisture, and flavor better than conventional ovens. Ounin steam ovens also offer combination modes that blend steam with traditional convection heat for optimal results on everything from bread to roasted meats.

Regular ovens use only dry heat, which can dry out food during cooking. Steam ovens inject moisture into the cooking chamber, keeping food juicy and tender. Steam also conducts heat more efficiently than hot air, allowing food to cook faster at lower temperatures. This results in better texture, enhanced flavor retention, and higher nutrient preservation--especially beneficial for vegetables, fish, and baked goods.

Ounin steam ovens offer a wide temperature range to handle any cooking task: Steam mode operates from 100--F - 30--F (40--C - 10--C) for gentle cooking, proofing, and sous vide-style results. Convection mode reaches up to 450--F (230--C) for roasting and baking. Combination modes blend both for versatile cooking at temperatures ranging from 210--F - 50--F (100--C - 30--C).

Using Your Oven

Ounin ovens feature a smooth, non-porous stainless steel interior that resists staining and odors. For daily cleaning, simply wipe with a damp cloth. For stubborn residue, run a steam cleaning cycle: fill the reservoir with a mixture of water and white vinegar (50/50), select the Steam Clean function, and run for 30 minutes. Let cool, then wipe clean. Never use abrasive cleaners, steel wool, or chemical descaling agents not approved by Ounin.

Descaling frequency depends on your water hardness and usage. If you use your steam oven daily, descale monthly. For moderate use (3 - times per week), descale every 2 - months. You'll know it's time when you notice longer heat-up times, less steam production, or white deposits around the interior. Use Ounin's official descaling solution or a mixture of equal parts water and white vinegar. The built-in descaling reminder will alert you when service is needed.
